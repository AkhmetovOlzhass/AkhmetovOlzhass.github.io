Frequently Asked Questions

Is smoking permitted on the Yacht? +
Yes, smoking is permitted on the outside porches of our yachts.
Are there water activities on Beno’s Yachts? +
Beno offers you an experience tailored to your needs. If you’re looking for some quiet time, you’ll be accommodated on yachts that offer just that and if you’re an avid adventurer, we also have water activities to turn up the excitement! Get in touch with your local Beno agent to find out if your rental can accommodate a slide, jet ski, and more.
What do I need to book a yacht? +
Original Passport Original Visit Visa
What forms of payment do you accept? +
Cash, Credit, as well as Apple Pay.
Is it compulsory to wear a life jacket on the yacht? +
No, wearing a life jacket on our yachts is a personal choice but if you can’t swim, this just might be a good idea.
